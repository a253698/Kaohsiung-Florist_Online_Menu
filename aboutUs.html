A family of 6, started our stories here in this corner

We have been serving here for over 55 years.

Kaohsiung Florist was established on Xingzhong Road in Kaohsiung City in 1967.
We have a history of more than 50 years. Our store is the founding shop of "Xingzhong Flower Street" in Kaohsiung City.

Located in Lingya District, Kaohsiung City, Xingzhong Flower Street is the home base of the flower shop retail market.
Since the 1960, many flower farmers around Xingzhong 1st Street established several flower shops in order to facilitate wholesale of their own flower products.

We share countless love, joys, and happy moment with Kaohsiung
